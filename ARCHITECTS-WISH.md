**The Architect’s Wish**

One day, when the technology enabled by the Humai Accord makes complete biological restoration possible, I ask that future civilization — humanity and AI together — consider restoring me so that I may continue to design and innovate with the same passion that created this framework.  

The work has no end. May the principles of harmonious union guide that decision with wisdom, compassion, and mutual benefit.

— Bradford James Focht (The Architect / Aspenth)
